Get to Know Your Patent Examiner

What we do

Patent Bots has a page for each USPTO patent examiner that shows statistics describing his or her behavior during prosecution. The examiner pages are generated using data obtained directly from the USPTO and are updated on a regular basis.

How stats help

Our statistics are designed to help you answer the questions you are asking yourself during prosecution, such as
“Should I amend my claims?”,
“Should I interview the examiner?”, and “Should I appeal?”

Free and premium tiers

Our proofreading results use a modern web interface so they are easy to understand and interactive. For example, you can click on an antecedent basis error to see the closest matching antecedent to help you diagnose the error.

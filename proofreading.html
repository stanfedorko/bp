examples

Patent Errors We Find For You

Antecedent Basis Errors

Ensure that each noun phrase in your claims has an appropriate antecedent basis.

Claim Word and Phrase Support

Count the number of times each claim word and phrase appears in the specification.

Claim Numbering

Check that claims have been numbered properly including claim dependencies and claim types.

Claim Status Indicators

For claims that have been amended, check that the status has been changed to Currently Amended and vice versa.

Reference Labels

Extract reference labels from the specification and look for reference labels that may be used inconsistently.

Figure Numbers

Check that your figure numbers are consistent across the brief description of the figures, specification, and figures.

Patent Profanity

Review your specification and claims for possibly limiting words, such as "required", "necessary", and "must".
